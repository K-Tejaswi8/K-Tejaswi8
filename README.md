### 🚀 Featured Projects

#### 🔍 [Job Market Analysis — Naukri.com EDA](https://github.com/Tejaswi-k8912/Job-Market-Analysis)
`Python` `Selenium` `Pandas` `Matplotlib` `Seaborn` `SciPy`

Revealed IT as the top-hiring sector, Hybrid as the dominant work mode, and identified Hyderabad and Bengaluru as leading hiring hubs — with salary benchmarking recommendations.

#### 📊 [Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard](https://github.com/Tejaswi-k8912/Customer_Churn_Power_BI)
`Power BI` `DAX` `Power Query` `Excel`
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing telecom customer churn across demographics, billing, and contract data. Developed DAX KPIs — **Churn Rate, Revenue Loss, Avg. Tenure, Avg. Monthly Charges** — to quantify attrition's business impact and drive retention strategy.

#### 🎬 [Movie Ticket Booking System](https://github.com/Tejaswi-k8912/Movie-Ticket-Booking-System-using-Python)
`Python` `OOP`
Built an end-to-end ticket booking system using OOP principles (classes, inheritance, polymorphism). Features seat allocation with **double-booking prevention**, dynamic cost calculation across multiple payment methods, and a full transaction flow from selection to confirmation.

#### 📈 FII Impact on Indian Stock Market (BSE)
`Python` `Pandas` `Excel` `Power BI`
Analyzed **9 years (2016–2024)** of BSE and Foreign Institutional Investor data to study the correlation between FII flows and market movement. Cleaned and merged multi-year datasets in Python and Excel, computed correlation metrics, and delivered stakeholder-ready Power BI dashboards.
