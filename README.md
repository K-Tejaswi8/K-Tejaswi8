## 📌 Featured Projects

### 🔍 [Job Market Analysis](https://github.com/Tejaswi-k8912/Job-Market-Analysis)
`Python` `Selenium` `Pandas` `Matplotlib` `Seaborn` `SciPy`
Scraped 2,000+ real-time job postings from Naukri.com and performed univariate, bivariate, and multivariate analysis with hypothesis testing — uncovering IT as the top-hiring sector and Hybrid as the dominant work mode.

### 📊 [Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard](https://github.com/Tejaswi-k8912/Customer_Churn_Power_BI)
`Power BI` `DAX` `Power Query` `Excel`
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing telecom customer churn, with DAX-driven KPIs (Churn Rate, Revenue Loss, Avg. Tenure) to quantify the business impact of attrition.

### 🎬 [Movie Ticket Booking System](https://github.com/Tejaswi-k8912/Movie-Ticket-Booking-System-using-Python)
`Python` `OOP`
A Python ticket booking system built on OOP principles (classes, inheritance, polymorphism) with double-booking prevention and dynamic cost calculation.

### 📈 FII Impact on Indian Stock Market (BSE)
`Python` `Pandas` `Excel` `Power BI`
Analyzed 9 years (2016–2024) of BSE and Foreign Institutional Investor data to study correlation between FII flows and market movement, visualized through Power BI dashboards.
